DBCU ADMISSIONS

We warmly welcome junior high school completers, senior high school graduates, college transferees, second coursers, and foreign applicants to our campus.

Modes of Application and Enrollment for School Year 2025-2026

Online

  1. Go to login.dbcu.edu. Select New. and fill out the application form completely.
  2. Pay the reservation fee* online or over-the-counter thru our Alternative Payment Partners.
  3. Take a screenshot or a photo of your proof of payment and send it to the DBCU campus’ official Facebook messenger for validation.
  4. Prepare the necessary admission requirements for submission on or before the start of classes.

*Paid reservation fees will be deducted from the total assessment fees.

Onsite

Please see www.dbcu.html/about before visiting.

  1. Please visit Delmarva Beach Christian University with your requirements on hand.
  2. Fill out the application form provided.
  3. Pay the required amount indicated on the assessment form or the reservation fee at the Cashier’s window.
  4. Get the official receipt.
  5. Prepare the necessary admission requirements for submission on or before the start of classes.
